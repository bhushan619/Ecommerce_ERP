Product Description

This theme is provided by our partner

Valeo is our absolutely stunning, clean and modern medical WordPress theme, created for medical centers, clinics, health establishments, pharmacy or rehabilitation facilities. It was crafted by our best professionals in accordance with the highest industry standards. With this medical WordPress theme creating a personal website is a smooth process that doesn’t require any special skills or knowdlege.
The theme features all the necessary sections to become a highly effective website. The homepage witha full-width slider will quickly introduce you and your team to the clients. You have an appointment section, powered by the Booked plugin to keep track of the doctor’s meetings and make sure every appointment is held on time! The services section will represent everything you have to offer, so that your clients have a wide range of treatments to choose from. Having a personal website is a great platform for starting a blog, for this is the best way to make your patients feel like their doctor is always present and available to them any time! On top of that you have a gallery section to share the photos of your happy clients!

It’s powered by the Unyson framework and has a drag and drop page builder, which is a perfect way to create your own unique layouts without having to change the source code whatsoever. Lot’s of content modules and WordPress live customizer help to adjust the theme to your project requirements easily. Bootstrap 3 framework used as a code base guarantees smooth performance at any device and resolution.Integrated demo content helps to restore the exact copy of the preview version in a couple of minutes. The theme comes with great documentation file and really helpful support team that will be happy to help in case any questions pop up!
